FAQ

Common Questions

Do you list product pricing publicly?

No. Detailed product visibility and pricing are kept behind a secure sign-up and login flow to ensure only licensed, approved accounts have access to catalog details.

Who can request access?

Licensed clinics and wellness providers can request marketplace access via our registration page. Manufacturers, 503B facilities, resellers, and sales applicants can use the partner application form above.

Is PeakLifeRx a pharmacy?

No. PeakLifeRx is a technology-enabled procurement platform that connects licensed buyers with vetted supply partners through a centralized digital workflow. We do not manufacture or dispense medications.

How do reseller and sales applications work?

Use the partner application form on this page to submit your information. Our team will review your application and reach out within one business day to discuss fit, onboarding, and next steps.

How quickly can I get approved?

Most applications are reviewed and approved within 1 business day. Timelines can vary based on credential verification and account type, but we aim to keep the process fast and friction-free.

What products are available on the platform?

The full catalog — including GLP-1 compounded medications, peptides, vitamins, and wellness products — is available to approved accounts after login. Product visibility is kept behind authentication for compliance reasons.

Is there a cost to apply or join?

No. Creating an account and submitting an application is free. Our team will walk you through pricing and ordering details during the onboarding process.
