FAQ

Common Questions

Do you list product pricing publicly?

No. Detailed product visibility and pricing are kept behind a secure sign-up and login flow.

Who can request access?

Licensed clinics and wellness providers can request marketplace access. Manufacturers, 503B facilities, resellers, and sales applicants can use the partner form.

Is this a pharmacy?

No. PeakLifeRx is positioned as a technology-enabled procurement platform connecting licensed buyers and supply partners through a centralized workflow.

How do reseller and sales applications work?

Use the partner application form to submit your information. That creates an inbound path for reseller partnerships and independent sales opportunities.
